## User Persona: Medical Director

### Responsibilities: 
* Evaluating system-wide physician performance
* Having data driven discussions with physicians
* Crafting improvement plans with physicians
* Calculating physician payments


## User Stories/Workflows: 

### User Story 1:
As a Medical Director, I need to be able to analyze system-wide trends of physician performance, to better understand where improvements can be made.

##### Workflow:
* Physician Scorecard
* Review overall Quality Measure compliance vs. Targets
* Review overall PMPMâ€™s and Utilization vs. the Organization and Practice's PMPM and Utilization

### User Story 2:
As a Medical Director, I need to be able to have quick access to information to have an informed discussion with physicians about the areas that they are excelling at and where they have room for improvement.

##### Workflow:
* Physician Scorecard
* Search for and select the physician that you are meeting with
* Review their Quality Measure compliance rates vs. Target
* Review their Risk and Credibility adjusted PMPM values against their peers
* Review their Utilization against their peers

### User Story 3:
As a Medical Director, I need to be able to judge the performance of physicians relative to their peers in a fair and unbiased manner to determine physician compensation.

##### Workflow:
Shared Savings Tool
Add elements to the Shared Savings Tool Model and adjust weights
Review results
Print report to PDF or export to Excel
